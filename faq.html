Can planting trees solve climate change?

No, not by itself. Trees are a proven natural climate solution, but we must adhere to research and approach the problem from multiple perspectives. #PayungHijau is planting millions of trees to: 1) contribute to the solution, 2) convey a powerful message that this large community cares about the earth we call home, and 3) demonstrate that planting trees is a simple solution that anybody can support.

Who will plant the trees?

The trees will be planted by our organization with the help from other government and nongovernmental agencies
