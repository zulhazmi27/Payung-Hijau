About Us

Payung Hijau is a non-profit organization which purpose is to provide a channel for those who are inspired to contribute in our journey to restore the forests in our beloved country. The ecosystem of our forests are impacted alongside the growth of technological advances which puts the responsibility of maintaining and restoring these forests onto our shoulder. Payung Hijau started a movement in planting trees on affected forests and we would like to invite you into realizing our green vision. Hence, is why we are contributing to:

Sustainable Development Goals #15: Life on Land

Our nourishment and livelihoods are as much reliant on the earth as they are on the ocean. Plant life accounts for 80% of our diet, and agriculture is a vital economic resource for us. Forests encompass 30% of the Earth's surface, provide essential habitat for millions of species, are major sources of clean air and water, and are critical in the fight against climate change. To improve international food and water security, climate change mitigation and adaptation, and peace and security, immediate measures are required to minimize the loss of natural habitats and biodiversity, which are part of our common heritage.

Let us be the umbrella that shields our green forests!
